## Overview

Smart Junior is a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS) specifically engineered to modernize the operations of tutoring centers (Bimbel) serving students from elementary (SD) to high school (SMA) levels. This project demonstrates the capability to build a scalable, dual-interface platform that digitalizes the entire educational workflow, from administrative management to online content delivery. It showcases strong proficiency in structuring a system that meets the specific needs of both educational staff and young learners.

## Key Features

**Dual-Role Access and Management** : Implements secure access and distinct dashboards for both Mentors and Students, ensuring relevant functionality for each user group.
**"Mentor Content Repository** : Provides robust administrative tools for mentors to efficiently upload, organize, and manage diverse educational resources and online course materials.
**Centralized Student Data Management** : Enables mentors to track enrollment status, manage student data, and monitor overall performance and progress from a single interface.
**Collaborative Discussion Forum** : Features a dedicated discussion forum to facilitate real-time communication, Q&A sessions, and collaborative learning between students and mentors.
